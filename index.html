About us

We are a middle class couple from Louisville, Kentucky. Our interests are pool, food, and shopping. Louis travels to pool tournaments around the country. He also helps install Diamond pool tables. I work a full-time clerical night job.

Our first trip begins in Downtown Nashville Tennessee. We were both pretty stressed out with Covid, Money, Life. I am sure most people can relate. We had not been away somewhere that didn't involve pool for a long time, so my brilliant boyfriend, Louis, suggested we go to Nashville.

I planned the trip the week before. Which really just involved reserving a hotel room and looking up attractions in Nashvile. The following is our awesome adventure in Music City, U.S.A.

Please click on the links below to find out about the hotel,food, museums, and other attractions we enjoyed in Nashville.

For our stay, I chose the Drury Plaza Hotel Nasvhille Downtown. I compared several hotels online, but I liked the options and price of this hotel.

Hotel

It included free breakfast, happy hour drinks, and most importantly in Nashville, had attached parking. The breakfast was excellent, but carrying it back to the room for privacy was a pain. You were given 3 drinks for happy hour that you redeemed in an attached area to the bar. I chose a margarita which was excellent. There were 2 other options which I cannot recall. My boyfriend rarely drinks, so no help there. The attached parking garage was an additional $20.00 a night, but in Downtown Nashville, it was worth it.

I chose a king suite. It had a lounge area and bedroom separated by the large bathroom. The bathroom had a separate soaking tub and shower, as well as a toilet closet. The bed was comfortable and having separate areas was great since Lou wanted to sleep in. We had a small fridge, but to my extreme despair, no coffee maker.
